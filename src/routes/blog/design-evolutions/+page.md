## More Emoticons!

You see, there are only like 5 emoticons working in my basement at the moment, it's clearly not enough.
So in my quest to figuring out where to put more of these bastards an idea clicked my mind!
Why not use their ability to show emotions to show my own emotion per blog. 
**YES!** Mood based categories which can be indexed through emoticon's moods!
